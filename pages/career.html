Career

Training Coordinator

BASIS Institute of Technology & Management (BITM)

Dhaka, Bangladesh

About BITM

BASIS Institute of Technology & Management (BITM) is a premier ICT training institute that collaborates with BASIS member firms, JICA, JETRO, KOICA, and various government agencies to deliver training programs on cutting-edge and frontier technologies according to market demands.

Qualifications

  • At least a bachelor's degree in Engineering, Computer Science, or Information Communication Technology.

Experience

  • Minimum 07 years of experience, including 05 years in Training Management/Project Management.
  • Experience working with international training institutes and/or development agencies like JICA, KOICA, and the World Bank Group.
  • Experience working with Japanese, Koreans, and Americans is an added advantage.

Skills

  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Proficiency in Office Software and Communication Tools.
  • Experience in Classroom-Based Training, Online Training, and Learning Management Systems (LMS).

Job Description (JD)

  • Plan, coordinate, and oversee the delivery of training programs.
  • Liaise with trainers, instructors, and partners for course planning and execution.
  • Develop and manage course schedules, training materials, and logistics.
  • Build partnerships with BASIS member firms, academia, and international development partners.
  • Monitor and evaluate training program outcomes.
  • Prepare reports on training activities, budgets, and impact assessments.
  • Coordinate marketing and outreach for training programs.
  • Manage training-related databases and documentation.
  • Handle participant inquiries and resolve issues within SLA compliance.

What We Offer

  • Competitive salary and benefits package.
  • Opportunity to work with international partners and cutting-edge technologies.
  • Professional growth in a dynamic and collaborative environment.

Application Process

Interested candidates are requested to send their CV and a cover letter to md@bitm.org.bd and copy to info@bitm.org.bd by 10 February 2025. Please mention “Training Coordinator Application” in the subject line.

BITM is an equal opportunity employer and encourages applications from qualified individuals regardless of gender, ethnicity, or background.
